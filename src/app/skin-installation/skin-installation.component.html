Installing

Windows

  • Download: friends.custom.css & ofriends.custom.css from above customiser or other sources
  • Move: friends.custom.css & ofriends.custom.css to Steam/clientui

Linux & macOS

  • Download: webkit.css from above customiser or other sources
  • Move: webkit.css to Steam/skins/"skinName"/resource
  • You might want to combine with the webkit.css that is already there rather than replace it. This would keep styles from your main client skin but could cause issues
  • Does not need the patcher

Steam Beta

If you are on the current steam beta please follow these instructions

  • Download: friends.custom.css & ofriends.custom.css from above customiser or other sources
  • Move: friends.custom.css & ofriends.custom.css to Steam/steamui
  • Add the following imports to: Steam/steamui/library.custom.css.
    @import url(./friends.custom.css);
    @import url(./ofriends.custom.css);
